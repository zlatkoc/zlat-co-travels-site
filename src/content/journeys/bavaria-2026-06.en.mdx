A conference was the excuse — two days of event modeling in Munich. What the week actually turned
out to be about was water: the Isar full of people escaping a record June heatwave, surfers queuing
on a river wave in the middle of a city, and, on the last morning, a lake so still and green it
felt like a different country.

Two parts: the city, and the lake.
